Refund Policy

At Tastika, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We put love and care into every jar of pickle, but if you receive a damaged or incorrect product, we’re here to help.

You can request a refund or replacement within 7 days of delivery. To be eligible, please ensure the product is unused, sealed, and in its original packaging.

Refunds will only be processed for genuine cases such as damaged, expired, or incorrect orders. We do not accept refund requests for taste preferences or opened jars due to hygiene reasons.

Once your request is approved, refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

To initiate a refund, contact our support team at tastikaonline@gmail.com with your order ID, photos, and issue details.

We appreciate your understanding and thank you for being a part of the Tastika family.
