Shipping Policy

At Tastika, we take great care to ensure that your favorite homemade pickles reach you fresh, flavorful, and safely packaged. We currently ship across India with reliable delivery partners.

Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days after placement. Shipping times may vary depending on your location, but we strive to deliver within 3–7 working days.

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation message with your tracking details.

If your package arrives damaged or delayed, please contact us at tastikaonline@gmail.com within 48 hours. We’ll resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Thank you for trusting Tastika — bringing the authentic taste of Indian tradition to your doorstep!
